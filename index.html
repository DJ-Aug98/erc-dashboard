Impact
Measuring impact remains a challenge for the ERC. With 93.2% of visitors anonymous, the platform cannot yet track downstream outcomes — which educators applied strategies, for which students, or with what results. Direct impact data is not available from current systems. What is observable: educators engaged at depth sufficient to register, save content, and cite evidence.
